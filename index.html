The Evolution of the Web

The World Wide Web, created by Sir Tim Berners-Lee, has revolutionized modern life. It has transformed the way people do business, communicate, and access information. This website explores its history, its impact, and the crucial role of HTML.

Key Terms

Term Definition
HTML HyperText Markup Language, used to structure web pages.
